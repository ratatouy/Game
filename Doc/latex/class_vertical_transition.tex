\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Vertical \mbox{\hyperlink{class_transition}{Transition}} to a neighboring scene. 

A Vertical \mbox{\hyperlink{class_transition}{Transition}} is essentially a line caracterized by two points on the same x axis That activates if the player is between or past those two points. The \mbox{\hyperlink{class_transition}{Transition}} can either go up or go down. Usually the transition is placed on the top or bottom border of the scene.
