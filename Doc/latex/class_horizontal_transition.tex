\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Horizontal \mbox{\hyperlink{class_transition}{Transition}} to a neighboring scene. 

A Horizontal \mbox{\hyperlink{class_transition}{Transition}} is essentially a line caracterized by two points on the same y axis That activates if the player is between or past those two points. The \mbox{\hyperlink{class_transition}{Transition}} can either go left or go right. Usually the transition is placed on the left or right border of the scene.
